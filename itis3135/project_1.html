Requirements & Design Proposal



Project Overview


Description

Our application is a website for AGM Media Marketing Agency, which specializes in promoting clients' businesses using social media platforms. The website's primary purpose is to provide information about the services offered by AGM Media, showcase their portfolio, and facilitate client inquiries and engagement.

Intended Users

  • Potential Clients: Businesses looking for social media marketing services.
  • Existing Clients: Clients who want to access their account information and communicate with AGM Media.
  • Admins: Staff responsible for managing content, client accounts, and inquiries.

Content Overview

The website will feature the following content:

  1. Home Page
    • Overview of AGM Media's services and mission.
    • Testimonials from satisfied clients.
    • Contact information and inquiry form.
  2. Services
    • Detailed descriptions of the social media marketing services offered.
    • Pricing information.
  3. Portfolio
    • Showcase of successful social media campaigns and client work.
    • Client success stories and case studies.
  4. Client Portal
    • User registration and login for existing clients.
    • Access to account information, including campaign performance reports.
    • Communication channels with AGM Media.
  5. Contact Us
    • Contact form for general inquiries.
    • Business location and contact details.

Dynamic Functionality on the Website


I plan to implement the following dynamic functionalities using JavaScript:

  • Inquiry Form Validation: Implement client-side validation for the contact and inquiry forms to ensure that users provide valid and complete information before submission.
  • Service Pricing Calculator: Allow potential clients to estimate the cost of services based on their requirements using an interactive calculator.

These dynamic features will improve user engagement, streamline communication, and provide a modern and interactive experience for both potential and existing clients of AGM Media Marketing Agency.
